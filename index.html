Valorant is a 5v5 multiplayer first-person shooter (FPS) where one team attacks and the other defends. The main game mode, Search and Destroy, is very similar to CS:GO. The attacking team’s goal is to plant a bomb (called a spike) and have it detonate, while the defending team tries to avoid that. Regardless of whether the spike is planted or not, if a squad is wiped out before any other victory condition is met, the opposing squad will win. a 5v5 multiplayer first-person shooter (FPS) where one team attacks and the other defends. Best Peripherals

For Valorant, gamers use laptop keyboards all the way to the Logitech G PRO X. If you have just started playing, you should use something like a Razer Ornata. For a good mouse, you should use a Razer Deathadder, a Logitech G502 Hero, or Logitech G Pro X Superlight.
